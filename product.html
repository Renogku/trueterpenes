Storage Requirements

Keep in original container, keep refrigerated for optimal freshness, lid securely tightened and away from heat, sunlight, open flames and hot surfaces. Store in a cool dry place.

Dilution

Terpene products must be diluted prior to use. Dilute between 1%–5% by weight depending on application. Terpenes are oil soluble.

Handle With Care

May cause skin irritation. Do not use prior to dilution.

This product is a concentrated material and must be diluted prior to use. Avoid contact with skin and eyes. Do not ingest undiluted.

Keep away from heat, sparks, open flames and hot surfaces. Use only in well ventilated areas.

This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease.
