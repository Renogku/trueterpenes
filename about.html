Extraordinary Sensory Journeys to Enhance the Human Experience

As a next-generation flavor house with an innovative terpene solutions platform, we create incredible sensory experiences under the industry's highest safety standards. We are creators, scientists, and outside-the-box thinkers. We champion the terpene kingdom with science-based research and leverage sensory analysis to create flavors and aromas that are true to experience.

Simplify the Complexity with an End-to-End Terpene Solutions Partner

Gain a competitive advantage. Partner with True Terpenes to create a New Product Development and terpene applications strategy to drive your business forward.

Safety

We provide an unmatched level of safety and quality to ensure that you have everything you need to create best selling products with confidence.

Innovation

We are a one-stop shop for ingredients, solutions, and application expertise. Our commercial and operations teams collaborate with you to meet needs throughout the entire product life-cycle.

Consistency

Source from the first terpene company with ISO 9001: 2015, GMP, and FSSC 22000 third-party certification to elevate your brand portfolio and standardize products across state lines and globally.

Leaders in Flavor

From true craft cannabis flavor grown on our hemp farm to bold and juicy fruit flavors to authentic botanical recreations of iconic and trending cannabis strains, we've built the industry's most compelling portfolio of flavors.

Our unique sensory-driven approach is a blend of art, science, and innovation. We combine the most powerful tools from traditional flavor formulation, advanced analytics, and our own hemp genetics program.
